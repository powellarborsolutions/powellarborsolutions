Don't Wait on Necessary Tree Work

Tree Work Is Often Unexpected

A storm rolls through, a pine drops a limb on your fence, or your arborist flags a hazardous oak during a routine inspection — tree work doesn't always happen on a convenient timeline. Large removals, emergency response, and defensible space projects can add up quickly, especially on larger foothill properties with mature trees.

Financing can help you move forward with the work that needs to happen now, rather than deferring a hazard removal or pushing back critical fire season prep. Through our financing partner Wisetack, qualifying homeowners throughout Placer and Nevada County can check available options with no commitment required — and no impact to your credit score just for checking.

After you receive your estimate, we can help point you toward the pre-qualification link. Or check your options anytime — before your project, before your estimate, or whenever it's convenient.
