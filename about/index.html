Our Story

About Powell Arbor Solutions

Powell Arbor Solutions is a locally owned tree service company serving Nevada County, Placer County, and surrounding Sierra foothill communities. Built on hard work, word-of-mouth, and a commitment to doing things the right way, our focus has always been safe, professional tree care you can rely on.

2015

How It All Started

Powell Arbor Solutions began in early 2015 with a small truck, a dump trailer, and a strong work ethic. What started as a simple local operation has grown into a trusted tree service company serving the surrounding foothill communities.

The Team

Meet David & Kayla Powell

Powell Arbor Solutions is owned and operated by David and Kayla Powell.

David is an ISA Certified Arborist with over 20 years of experience in climbing, tree care, and hazard mitigation. He leads all field operations with a strong focus on safety, precision, and proper arboricultural practices.

Kayla has been part of the business since the beginning, helping build and support the company behind the scenes while also playing a growing role in operations and customer communication.

Operations

How We Work Today

While David leads field operations, Kayla has traditionally worked behind the scenes managing operations, organization, and business development. Over time, she has begun stepping into a more client-facing role, handling incoming calls, customer intake, and assisting with estimate coordination.

She will also occasionally join David on property walkthroughs and estimates, helping bridge communication between the field and the customer experience.

This structure allows the business to stay responsive, organized, and closely connected to every project from start to finish.

Growth

Built Into What We Are Today

From a small truck and trailer to a fully equipped professional tree service operation, Powell Arbor Solutions has grown steadily over the years through hard work, repeat customers, and community trust.

Today, we are equipped to handle everything from residential tree care to large-scale HOA and commercial projects while maintaining the same values we started with.

The Future

Looking Ahead

We're proud of how far we've come, and even more focused on where we're going. Our goal is to continue improving service quality, expanding capabilities, and serving our local communities for many years to come.

Work With a Local Team You Can Trust

If you're looking for experienced, professional tree care from a local company that values safety, honesty, and quality work — we'd be happy to help.
