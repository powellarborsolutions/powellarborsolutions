What We Do

Tree Services in Grass Valley & Surrounding Areas

Nevada and Placer Counties are home to some of California's most beautiful — and most demanding — tree environments. Ponderosa pines, valley oaks, black oaks, and incense cedars define the region, but they also bring real challenges: storm damage, wildfire risk, root intrusion, and structural hazards near homes and property lines.

From mature oaks near residential foundations in Grass Valley, to creek-side removals in Auburn, to tight-lot properties in Nevada City with aging pine stands — Powell Arbor Solutions provides safe, professional tree care tailored to each property.

Tree Removal

Safe, efficient removal of hazardous, dead, or unwanted trees. We handle steep terrain, limited access, and large-scale projects with precision rigging techniques that protect your property from start to finish.

Learn more

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Expert pruning that promotes tree health, improves structure, reduces wildfire fuel load, and keeps your property looking its best. Thoughtful canopy management performed by a certified arborist, not just a chainsaw crew.

Learn more

Tree Planting & Installation

Thoughtful selection and professional installation of the right tree for the right spot. We consider soil, exposure, mature size, and fire-wise spacing so your new trees thrive for decades in the foothills.

Learn more

Plant Health Care

Diagnosis and treatment of pests, disease, and decline in mature trees. Soil evaluation, pruning for vigor, and proactive care plans that extend the life of the valuable trees on your property.

Learn more

Defensible Space (Fire Prevention)

Cal Fire–aligned fuel reduction, ladder-fuel removal, and strategic canopy thinning to protect your home. We help Nevada and Placer County property owners meet defensible space requirements and insurance expectations.

Learn more

Arborist Reports & Consultations

Written reports for permits, real estate transactions, insurance claims, HOA disputes, and pre-construction tree protection. Certified, defensible documentation from ISA Arborist David Powell (#WE-8944A).

Learn more

Storm Damage Cleanup

Fallen limbs, split trunks, and wind-thrown trees cleared quickly and safely. We coordinate with insurance when needed and fully clean the site — from the roof line down to the sawdust.

Learn more

Stump Grinding & Removal

Stump grinding is the process of removing a tree stump below ground level using specialized grinding equipment to clear the area for safety, aesthetics, or future landscaping. Available upon request.

Learn more
