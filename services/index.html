Specialty Support

Insurance Claim Support

When you choose us for emergency tree work related to storm damage or hazardous conditions, we can work directly with your insurance carrier to support the claims process.

We provide detailed documentation, photos, and written estimates to help your adjuster clearly understand the scope of work required. Once a claim is active, we coordinate directly with your insurance provider to complete approved work safely and efficiently.

Please note: insurance coverage typically applies to sudden damage events such as storms or fallen trees, and does not cover routine maintenance or preventative tree care.
