Storm Response

Nevada County Gets Hit Hard — We Know What to Do

The Sierra Nevada foothills experience intense winter storms that bring heavy snow, ice loading, and powerful wind events — conditions that can bring down entire trees or snap major limbs with little warning. When a storm hits Grass Valley, Nevada City, Auburn, or surrounding communities, the call volume spikes fast. Powell Arbor Solutions maintains 24/7 availability for these events and has been responding to storm damage across the region for over 20 years.

Storm-damaged trees present a different set of challenges than routine removal. A tree that has partially fallen may be under significant tension or compression — making cuts in the wrong location dangerous. Hanging limbs ("widow makers") lodged in the canopy after wind events are a serious hazard that require trained eyes and the right rigging equipment to address safely. ISA Certified Arborist David Powell brings the expertise to assess, prioritize, and safely resolve each situation.

Beyond emergency response, we also handle non-emergency storm cleanup — properties that sustained damage during a storm but don't require immediate response. After any major storm event in Nevada or Placer County, it's worth having your trees assessed for hidden structural damage, broken branch unions, or compromised root systems that may not be visible from the ground. We offer written arborist reports for insurance documentation and permit purposes.

What's Included

Complete Storm Damage Response — Safe, Thorough, Documented

From the initial hazard assessment to final debris removal, we handle the full scope of storm tree cleanup.

Hazard Assessment ISA Certified Arborist evaluates the scene for secondary hazards — hanging limbs, tension wood, root upheaval, and proximity to utilities — before any cutting begins.
Safe Tree Removal Under Tension Storm-fallen trees require careful tension-relief cuts. We use proper rigging and sectional techniques to safely process trees that are pinned, split, or partially uprooted.
Hanging Limb Removal We locate and safely remove all widow makers — broken limbs lodged in the canopy that pose a delayed hazard long after a storm has passed.
Structure Clearing When trees have landed on roofs, fencing, vehicles, or outbuildings, we work carefully to remove the tree without causing additional structural damage.
Complete Debris Removal All branches, wood, and debris are removed from your property. We can leave firewood-length cuts on-site or haul everything away — your choice.
Insurance Documentation We provide written documentation of storm damage for insurance purposes. If an arborist report is needed for your claim, we can provide a formal written assessment.
